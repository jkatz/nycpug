Tickets for PGConf US 2016 on April 18-20, 2016 are available through EventBrite.   The first day, April 18, is dedicated to training sessions and special purpose summits; these are separate purchases from the standard conference pass.  The main events for PGConf US 2016 take place from April 19-20 and can be purchased above as well.

If you are a student and interested in attending PGConf US 2016, we are offering a discount rate of $49 for admission. Please email pgconf@postgresql.us to register. You must be an active student at the time of the conference.

PGConf US 2016 is hosted by the United States PostgreSQL Association, which is a registered 501(c)(3). All ticket sales go directly to the program of PGConf US 2016 and any proceeds are used for the ongoing activities of the United States PostgreSQL Association, which include sponsoring PostgreSQL meetup groups, providing educational materials for PostgreSQL, and sponsoring speakers to discuss PostgreSQL at various events.

If you have any questions, please reach out to us at pgconf@postgresql.us
